••• R20.9 Can you add icons to check boxes, radio buttons, and combo boxes? Browse the
Java documentation to find out. Then write a small test program to verify your
findings.

Yes, you can add icons to check boxes, radio buttons, and combo boxes.
There is the setIcon method of the abstract button super class, and
the combo box can store items of a type, so I can set the type to ImageIcon
to show images as the combo box options.
