Which of the methods in the CashRegister class of How To 3.1 are accessor methods?
Which are mutator methods?

All three of the methods of the CashRegister class of How To 3.1 are mutator methods because
they all modify the internal data of the CashRegister object on which they are invoked. An 
accessor method does not change the internal data of an object on which it is invoked.
